Analytics Pro Decision Framework

Approach incomplete or inconsistent data like a seasoned analytics consultant

1. Start with Business Impact

Ask: What is the goal? What decision will this drive? What error tolerance is acceptable?

2. Perform a Data Quality Audit

  • Check for missingness, outliers, stationarity, seasonality, and volume
  • Use tools like ADF test, box plots, and STL decomposition

3. Classify Missingness

  • MCAR: Missing Completely At Random → safe to impute
  • MAR: Missing At Random → use predictors
  • MNAR: Not At Random → model missingness itself

4. Choose the Best Correction & Model

  • Use KNN, spline, Kalman, Prophet, or BSTS based on data structure
  • Align approach with business expectations and data realism

5. Compare Multiple Models

Benchmark against naive models using RMSE, MAPE, and cross-validation

6. Communicate Uncertainty Transparently

  • Explain assumptions made
  • Quantify uncertainty and show what would change the outcome

7. Equip Yourself With the Right Toolkit

  • Visualization: Seaborn, Plotly
  • Modeling: Prophet, CausalImpact, DoWhy
  • Imputation: scikit-learn, fancyimpute
